## Overview
[cite_start]Successfully defended at the University of Minnesota (November 2025), this thesis explores the intersection of application requirements and distributed infrastructure capabilities[cite: 46, 47, 48].

## Core Research Areas
* **Design principles for Edge Storage:** Proposed a set of design principles for researching building edge native storage systems.
* **Data placement:** Developed data placement strategies optimizing replica storage based on network latency and demand geolocation.
* **Data prefetching:** Developed a prefetching strategy for edge-native applications like AR that uses temporal and spatial awareness in conjunction with associations to prefetch 3D objects.
* **Data reduction:** Developed a sensor selection and sampling strategy for extending wearable mean time between recharges.
