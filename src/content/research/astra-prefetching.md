## Abstract
ASTRA addresses the latency bottlenecks in edge-assisted Augmented Reality (AR) by modeling user intent through field-of-view and spatial data.

*Note: This research received the Best Research Paper Award at IEEE IC2E 2025*.

## Methodology & Results
Built utilizing C++ and SimCpp, the predictive framework dynamically anticipates data needs.
