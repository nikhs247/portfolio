## Abstract
Viveka proposes a novel framework for managing multimodal sensor fusion on platforms with severe resource constraints. This research forms the theoretical foundation currently being transitioned into defense applications.

## Methodology & Results
* **Adaptive Algorithms:** Developed adaptive algorithms to balance sensor selection dynamically alongside sampling rates.
* **Efficiency Gains:** The approach achieved 62% energy savings and a 74% reduction in data transmission.
